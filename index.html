Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of functional analysis and partial differential equations, with particular emphasis on Sobolev spaces and variational methods.

Functional Analysis

Hilbert/Banach spaces, operator theory, spectral theory, and their applications in PDEs.

Partial Differential Equations

Elliptic, hyperbolic, and Hamilton-Jacobi equations with focus on existence and regularity.

Sobolev Spaces

Embedding theorems, trace inequalities, and applications in variational problems.

Calculus of Variations

Direct methods, Γ-convergence, and applications in material science and physics.

Geometric Measure Theory

Rectifiable sets, currents, and applications in minimal surfaces and PDEs.

Fourier Analysis

Harmonic analysis, pseudodifferential operators, and applications in signal processing.
