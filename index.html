About Me

I am a BS-MS Mathematics major at IISER Mohali with research experience in functional analysis, partial differential equations, Sobolev spaces, geometric measure theory, and calculus of variations.

My work spans theoretical analysis and computational approaches, with applications to elliptic PDEs, Hamilton–Jacobi equations, scalar conservation laws, and operator theory.

I aspire to pursue graduate-level research in pure mathematics with a focus on PDEs and analysis.
